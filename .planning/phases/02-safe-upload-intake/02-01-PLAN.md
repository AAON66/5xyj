Change `_store_upload()` to accept `max_upload_size_bytes` from `create_import_batch()`, accumulate `file_size` per chunk, and raise the dedicated exception immediately when the streamed bytes cross the limit even when `content-length` is wrong or missing. On overflow, unlink the partial file inside `_store_upload()` before re-raising; then reuse the existing `create_import_batch()` rollback to delete the batch directory and persisted `ImportBatch` row. Do not redesign middleware buffering, route shapes, region detection, parse sequencing, progress payload shapes, or dual-template export orchestration.</action>

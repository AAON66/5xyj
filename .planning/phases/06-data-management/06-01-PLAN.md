- `get_period_summary(db, region=None, company_name=None, page=0, page_size=20) -> PaginatedPeriodSummaryRead`:
  GROUP BY billing_period. Use func.count() for total_count, func.sum() for totals, func.avg() for averages. Apply region/company filters. Sort by billing_period DESC.
